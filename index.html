Instructions

1. Choose your difficulty level: Easy, Fair, or Hard.
2. Click the "Start" button to begin.
3. Control your character:

- Use the left arrow key to move left.

- Use the right arrow key to move right.

4. Your goal is to avoid colliding with opponents:

- In Easy mode, avoid Queen.

- In Fair mode, avoid King.

- In Hard mode, avoid Kaido.

5. Each successful avoidance earns you a score.
6. To return to the main page, click the "Bounty Rush" image at the top-left corner of the page.
